Frequently Asked Questions

How long does bookings last?

All bookings last up to one hour. After that time that particular room can be booked by another person and paid for.

What are the payment methods?

This platform currently supports three(3) forms of payment.

  • Online payment with mobile money for all networks
  • Online payment with bank card
  • Physical payments to a specified bank account

How long does my verification code last?

All verification codes(V-code) last till the end of an academic year. After expiration users can update their V-code on the platform for another academic year.
